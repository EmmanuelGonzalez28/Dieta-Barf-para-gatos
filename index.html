What is the Barf Diet?

Barf Diet

The B.A.R.F Diet is the practice of feeding dogs, cats, and other domestic animals a diet primarily composed of raw foods.

Why is raw feeding suitable for cats?

This feeding method is suitable mainly because cats are carnivorous by nature. A raw food diet gives them just what they need: plenty of fresh meat. Unlike food cooked at home, the BARF diet is offered raw, without cooking or grilling.

Features

100% natural and delicious

Balanced diet with homemade food for human consumption. The cat will have a correct proportion of the food that is needed for a balanced diet

Diet balanced in protein

The cat will administer a diet that provides a high proportion of protein and fiber, and low in carbohydrates and fats

Improve the health of your michi

Considerably reduces weight problems and laziness. His coat will be shiny and healthy. The bad smell of feces will no longer be a problem.
